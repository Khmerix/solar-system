MISSION TUTORIAL
Mission Control Briefing
Read this quick guide before starting. Choose the view below for Students or Teachers.

Game Goal

  • Explore planets and special bodies like the Sun and Black Hole.
  • Complete missions, quizzes, and events to earn XP and level up.
  • Unlock badges and build mission streaks for bigger rewards.

How To Play

  • Click a planet or use Quick Jump on the right panel.
  • Read the planet card, then use Start Mission or Take Quiz.
  • Follow the live mission strip at the bottom for objective + timer.
  • Use Time Speed and view toggles to adapt during missions.

Tips To Win

  • Navigation mission: get close and hold position.
  • Observation mission: keep target centered.
  • Survival mission: maintain speed and avoid black hole danger zones.

Classroom Use

  • Use as a warm-up, station activity, or review game.
  • Students practice science vocabulary while solving timed objectives.
  • Quizzes provide quick formative checks per planet.

Suggested Lesson Flow (20-30 min)

  • 5 min: Intro and demonstration of controls.
  • 10-15 min: Student mission + quiz rotation.
  • 5-10 min: Debrief using Event Feed and discovered facts.

Assessment Ideas

  • Track completion: missions, quizzes, badges, and level progression.
  • Ask students to compare inner vs outer planet mission difficulty.
  • Use mission reflections: strategy used, errors made, and science facts learned.
